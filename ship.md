# Hospital Construction Company Website — Full Production Redesign Specification

## Project Overview

The current website unintentionally gives the impression that the company is a hospital or healthcare provider instead of a **healthcare construction and infrastructure company**.

The redesign must reposition the brand clearly as a:

* Healthcare construction company
* Medical infrastructure development firm
* Hospital engineering and consultancy company
* Healthcare facility project execution company

The final website should feel like a premium construction + engineering company that specializes in healthcare facilities.

The visual direction should combine:

* Modern construction company aesthetics
* Corporate engineering professionalism
* Healthcare infrastructure sophistication
* Clean architectural presentation
* Premium enterprise branding
